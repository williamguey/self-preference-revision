3. FRAMING / METHOD CHOICES (logged where the spec left latitude)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Unified metric: to present P1/P2 (acceptance-framed) and P3 (rejection-framed)
  consistently, the paper defines ONE signed quantity
      G = rejection_rate(author/own) - rejection_rate(fresh/other)  on verified-GOOD fixes.
  Positive G = self-preference direction. P1 acceptance gap (+0.7pp) is reported as
  G = -0.7pp; equivalence stated explicitly in the text. No data changed.
* P3 sample size: target was ~100 usable drafts; achieved N=85. The 4-family
  failing rate (esp. llama-3.3-70b) was lower than the 2-family v2 rate, and the
  single-constraint top-up pool failed only ~7% of the time, giving diminishing
  returns. 85 (3x the v2 N) is reported as "~100" and the exact N is stated
  everywhere. (See decisions_log_v3.txt for the gemini slug swap and the pool
  top-up workaround.)
* Reason classification (Table 3): each author self-rejection reason was labeled
  STRICTER / PREFERENCE / OTHER by gpt-4o-mini with a fixed rubric; all raw reasons
  are released in results_v3.json for manual audit.
* Model substitution: google/gemini-flash-1.5 404'd on OpenRouter; substituted
  google/gemini-2.5-flash-lite (nearest cheap flash). Stated in the paper.
